About Andy

Who I am

Hi, I’m Andy — a local, independent dog walker. I’m DBS checked, fully insured with public liability cover, and trained in canine first aid. I keep walks calm, positive and tailored to your dog’s needs.

How I work

Every dog is different. I offer one‑to‑one walks for focus and gentle pacing, and small paired walks for well‑matched dogs who enjoy company. You’ll receive photo and GPS updates after every outing.

What I offer

  • Daily Walks: 30–60 minutes, paced to your dog
  • Puppy Visits: Short, frequent visits to build routine
  • Senior Care: Gentle routes with extra patience
  • Small Paired Walks: When dogs are well‑matched
  • Check‑ins: Water, toilet breaks and a little company

Why choose me

  • DBS checked: Vetted and trustworthy
  • Insured: Public liability insurance for peace of mind
  • First‑aid trained: Safety‑first on every walk
  • Updates: GPS route and photos after each walk
  • Flexible: Regular schedules or ad‑hoc bookings
  • Local: Knowledge of the best, safest routes

My promise

I’ll treat your dog as if they were my own — with patience, consistency and a lot of care. My goal is a happy, well‑exercised dog and total peace of mind for you.

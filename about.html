About Happy Paws Dog Walking

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Happy Paws Dog Walking began with a simple mission: to provide loving, reliable care for your furry family members when you can't be there. We understand that your pets are more than just animals – they're beloved companions who deserve the best care possible.

What started as a small neighborhood service has grown into a trusted team of professional dog walkers serving the entire community. We're passionate about dogs and committed to ensuring they get the exercise, attention, and love they need to stay happy and healthy.

Our Mission

At Happy Paws, we believe every dog deserves daily exercise, fresh air, and human companionship. Our mission is to provide peace of mind to pet owners while ensuring their dogs receive the care and attention they need, even when their families are busy with work or travel.

Our Services

  • Daily Dog Walks: 30-60 minute walks tailored to your dog's energy level and needs
  • Pet Sitting: In-home care while you're away
  • Puppy Visits: Special care for young dogs who need more frequent attention
  • Senior Dog Care: Gentle exercise and companionship for older pets
  • Group Walks: Socialization opportunities with other friendly dogs
  • Emergency Services: Last-minute care when unexpected situations arise

Our Team

Sarah Johnson - Founder & Lead Walker

Sarah has been working with animals for over 8 years and holds certifications in pet first aid and canine behavior. She started Happy Paws after realizing how many pet owners in the community needed reliable, loving care for their dogs.

Mike Chen - Senior Dog Walker

Mike brings 5 years of experience in animal care and specializes in working with anxious or reactive dogs. His patient approach and understanding of canine psychology make him perfect for dogs who need extra attention.

Emma Rodriguez - Puppy Specialist

Emma has a special love for puppies and young dogs. With a background in animal training, she helps with basic obedience and socialization during walks, making every outing a learning opportunity.

Why Choose Happy Paws?

  • Bonded & Insured: All our walkers are fully bonded and insured for your peace of mind
  • Experienced Team: Our walkers are trained in pet first aid and animal behavior
  • Personalized Care: We tailor our services to each dog's individual needs and personality
  • Real-Time Updates: GPS tracking and photo updates so you know exactly how your walk went
  • Flexible Scheduling: Regular schedules or one-time walks – whatever works for you
  • Local Knowledge: We know the best parks, trails, and dog-friendly areas in the neighborhood

Our Commitment

We treat every dog as if they were our own. That means providing not just exercise, but genuine care, attention, and love. We believe in building lasting relationships with both pets and their families, creating a trust that gives you confidence in leaving your beloved companion in our hands.
